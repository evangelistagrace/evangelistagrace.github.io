_To mummy, Deborah and Sharon,_

We have laughed together,
We have shed tears together,
We have all seen dark days,
And we have all seen good days.

Through thick and thin,
We have had only each other's hands as our lifeline
Each other's shoulder to cry on,
And each other's presence to find solace in.

Now though we are miles apart,
We are still ever close at heart,
And may our physical distance
Serve as means to make our hearts grow fonder,
And love one another ceaselessly.

There may be an end to every physical distance
But to the love and bond that we share,
May it continue to blossom
Into a perpetual journey of love and affection.
